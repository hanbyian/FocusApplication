Welcome! This cool web application will help you stay focused and productive by turning it into a fun game. When you are ready to be productive with no distractions start the timer. Once you are done or succumb to distractions stop the timer. The longer you go being productive the higher your multiplier and the more points you get! Have fun, be productive!

Before you start a focus session take some time to think. What do I want to accomplish by the end of this session? What are things that might distract me? If you do something distracting or stop being productive you must stop your focus session timer.
